# App Pages (MVP)

## Onboarding
- Goal setup (books/year)
- Reading pace (minutes/day)
- Notification preferences

## Today
- Active book hero card
- Remaining minutes + chapter progress
- Daily reading plan

## Reader
- Full book summary
- 10 chapter summaries with context cards
- Resume from current chapter

## Library
- Continue Reading + Completed visibility
- Persistent history for unfinished and finished books

## Progress
- XP, streak, level
- Badge wall

## Profile
- Goal and notification settings
- Account sync state
